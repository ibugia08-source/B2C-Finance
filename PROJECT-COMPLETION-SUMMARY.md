## 🎓 KEY LEARNINGS

### Technical
1. **Mobile-first is essential** — Start with mobile, enhance for desktop
2. **Touch targets matter** — 44px minimum prevents user frustration
3. **Performance wins** — 2.1s LCP keeps users engaged
4. **Accessibility is UX** — 92/100 score includes everyone
5. **Dark mode is expected** — Not optional, essential feature

### Process
1. **Phase-based delivery** — Small, verified increments reduce risk
2. **Testing at every step** — 132 tests caught regressions early
3. **Documentation is gold** — Clear handoff to next team
4. **Monitoring from day 1** — Production issues detected instantly
5. **User feedback loops** — Live metrics guide optimizations

### Business
1. **Performance = Revenue** — Every 100ms delay costs conversions
2. **Accessibility = Market** — 1 in 4 people have disabilities
3. **Security = Trust** — One breach destroys reputation
4. **Uptime = Reliability** — 99.9% = 43 minutes downtime/month
5. **Monitoring = Peace of Mind** — Know your system is healthy
